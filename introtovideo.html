INTRODUCTION - PEER TO PEER INTERACTION

Peer to peer interaction is very important as it helps you network with your peers in the classroom. Through interacting with others in your class, develop important skills for groupwork and collaboration.

Learning Outcomes

Learn why it is important to build relationships with your peers
Gain tips and tricks for meeting new people and building your confidence
Hear real-world stories from your Inter-ACT guide
Practice your new skills in the classroom by completing the Inter-ACT Task
