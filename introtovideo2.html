INTRODUCTION - PEER AND TUTOR INTERACTION

Peer to tutor interaction is crucial for your learning so that you can get the most from your studies. Asking questions will help you to learn course material and is an important step to ensure you understand new content.

Learning Outcomes

Understand why it is important to ask questions in class
Learn how to ask a question which is clear and useful for your studies
Gain support from your Inter-ACT Guide on how to ask questions and learn from their experience
Practice asking questions in class by completing the Inter-ACT Task
