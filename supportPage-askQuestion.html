PEER AND TUTOR INTERACTION

Unable to complete the task? Don't worry, we are here to help you. Mistakes are proof that you are trying. Do not give up and try again. The video below has more tips which might help you to succeed.

Summary

  • It’s okay if you asked a question and you didn’t fully receive the information you’d hoped for.
  • You could make a note of your question for yourself and you could ask your tutor after class.
  • Your tutor or lecturer is there to support you so don’t be afraid to continue asking questions.
  • Stay curious about the topics you are covering in class to know which questions will be helpful to ask.
  • Don’t be afraid of asking questions! If you had the question, your classmates probably did too.
