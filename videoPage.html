PEER TO PEER INTERACTION

Watch the following video for tips on how to build great relationships with your friends in classroom. You will learn from an experienced student at QUT from your home country.

Summary

  • Making friends is important so that you can have support and fun during your studies in Australia.
  • To make new friends try to ask questions about people. Ask about what they are studying, or where they are from – try to find out more about them!
  • Try to find things in common between you and the other person to build a relationship and find similar interests.
  • Don’t be afraid to approach someone new and be confident. Sometimes you will need to make the first step to make a friend.
  • Keep practicing starting new conversations – practice makes perfect!
