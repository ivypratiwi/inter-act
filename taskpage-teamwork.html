TEAMWORK

TASK

Your task for Teamwork is to list the skills and abilities you would bring to a team. Remember, teamwork allows everyone to bring their strengths so it's important to understand the skills and abilities you will bring to your teams.

Tips from previous video

Working in a team means you can combine everyone’s strengths to create a different result than what you could develop on your own.
Remember, working in a team means that you work together and share the workload.
It’s important to know what you can bring to the team and then be confident in sharing your skills. Working in a team means you can make the best of everyone’s skills and abilities.
Don’t be afraid of teamwork! It is a great opportunity to learn and share new skills with your classmates.
