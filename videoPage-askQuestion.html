PEER AND TUTOR INTERACTION

Watch the following video for tips on how to ask questions to your tutor or lecturer in class. You will learn from an experienced QUT student who comes from your home country.

Summary

  • Asking questions is useful to learn new information or receive support fromyour tutor.
  • If you are struggling in class, asking a question is a good way to get back on track and move forward with your studies.
  • Before you ask your question, be clear on what it is that you want to find out or understand.
  • Prepare before your class so you know which areas you would like support in, so you are prepared to ask questions.
  • Try to stay focused in class so that you know which questions you would like to ask.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask questions – usually everyone else in the class has the same question!
