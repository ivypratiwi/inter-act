TEAMWORK

Unable to complete the task? Don't worry, we are here to help you. Mistakes are proof that you are trying. Do not give up and try again. The video below has more tips which might help you to succeed.

Summary

  • There may be disagreements or different ideas when you are working in a team and that’s okay!
  • But it’s important not to let these disagreements or differences of opinion grow bigger and bigger. Think of ways in which you could come up with a solution that is best for the team and the project.
  • Communication is important in a team, understand that everyone has their own ideas and it’s important to work together to work through challenges together.
  • Even though differences of opinion may seem like a challenge, this is where creativity can really flourish! Perhaps there is a different way of approaching the task that you didn’t think of before.
  • If there are challenges, try not to complain or blame your teammates. Instead, make sure you communicate with each other about what you might be struggling with so you can work together effectively.
